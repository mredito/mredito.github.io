Prakasam Barrage


                 

Accessibility

Transport:

Prakasam Barrage is located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. It connects the Krishna and Guntur districts and serves as a crucial source of irrigation, drinking water, and hydroelectric power.

Video

Road Condition

The roads leading to Prakasam Barrage are well-maintained and accessible year-round.

Best Time to Visit

Season

The ideal time to visit is from October to March when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing and enjoying the views of the barrage.

Attractions and Activities

Natural Beauty:

The barrage offers stunning views of the Krishna River and the surrounding landscape, making it a popular spot for photography and picnics.

Photography

Visitors can capture beautiful sunsets and the scenic beauty of the barrage and the river.

Picnicking:

There are several areas around the barrage suitable for picnicking. It's important to keep the environment clean.

Travel Tips

Guides:

Hiring a local guide can enhance your experience and help you learn more about the history and significance of the barrage.

Safety:

Always exercise caution near the water and follow any safety guidelines provided by local authorities.

Photography:

The barrage provides excellent opportunities for photography, especially during sunrise and sunset.

Nearby Attractions:

Kanaka Durga Temple:

A famous temple located on the Indrakeeladri hill, offering panoramic views of Vijayawada and the Krishna River.

Bhavani Island:

A popular tourist destination known for its lush greenery and water activities, accessible by boat from the barrage.
