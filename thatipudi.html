Thatipudi Reservoir


                 

Description:

Thatipudi Reservoir, located in the Vizianagaram district of Andhra Pradesh, is a significant source of irrigation and water supply for the surrounding regions.

Accessibility

The reservoir is easily accessible by road and is well connected to nearby towns.

Video:

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and the landscape is lush.

Attractions and Activities

Natural Beauty:

The reservoir offers breathtaking views of the water and surrounding hills, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Photography

Visitors can capture stunning sunsets and the picturesque landscape surrounding the reservoir.

Picnicking:

There are several scenic spots around the reservoir ideal for picnicking. Remember to keep the area clean.

Travel Tips

Guides:

Hiring a local guide can enhance your visit, providing insights into the history and ecology of the area.

Safety:

Always exercise caution near the water and follow any safety guidelines provided by local authorities.

Nearby Attractions:

Vizianagaram Fort:

This historic fort is a must-visit, known for its beautiful architecture and rich history.

Rani Durgavati Park:

A lovely park near the barrage, perfect for relaxing walks and enjoying the greenery.
