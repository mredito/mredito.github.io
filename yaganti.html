Yaganti Temple


                 

Description

The Yaganti Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is located in the Yaganti hills of Andhra Pradesh. It is renowned for its beautiful architecture and rich history, attracting devotees and tourists alike.

Built:

The temple was constructed during the 15th century under the rule of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Deity:

The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, worshipped here in the form of a Shivalinga.

Architectural Features

Style:

The temple showcases Dravidian architectural style, featuring intricate carvings and sculptures that depict various deities and mythological stories.

Main Shrine:

The main shrine houses the Shivalinga, which is believed to be growing in size, signifying the divine presence.

Intricate Carvings:

Visitors can admire beautiful carvings on the temple walls, showcasing scenes from Hindu mythology and the artistry of ancient craftsmen.
