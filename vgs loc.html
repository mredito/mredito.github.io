Location

From Vijayawada: Eluru is approximately 90 kilometers from Vijayawada. The route is well-connected by road, and you can reach Eluru via National Highway 16 (NH16) and State Highway 48 (SH48).

From Hyderabad: Eluru is around 350 kilometers from Hyderabad. You can travel by road using National Highway 65 (NH65).
